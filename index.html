Who we Are
sauti art rwanda

SAUTI ARTS RWANDA is non-government organization that brings together young adults aged 14-30 who identify as “SAUTI ARTS RWANDA” for intensive artistic training, leadership development and conflict solving on society, forming an artistic ensemble, we creat original multi-lingual performances based on our discoveries.
S A U TI A R T S R W A N D A
In SAUTI ARTS RWANDA we talk about the complexity of embracing individuality while honoring one’s family and community cultures. The ensemble creates compelling, high-quality performances that engage the public in dialog about critical social, culture, and generational issues, such as how racism, economic oppression, bigotry, media and violence can sever us from our culture origins. Our performances also invite audiences to celebrate our connection and our full humanity through the arts. The work of SAUTI ARTS RWANDA is rooted in our belief that our cultural legacies and family histories are sources of strength as well as struggle, as we shape our own lives, our communities, and our futures.
